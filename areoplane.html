Pakistan Airlines
Top 5 airlines of Pakistan are PIA, Serene, Airblue, Shaheen and Askari.Pakistan International Airlines is an international airline that serves as the national flag carrier of Pakistan under the administrative control of the Secretary to the Government of Pakistan for Aviation. PIA is Pakistan's largest airline and operates a fleet of 29 aircraft. The airline operates nearly 100 flights daily, servicing 18 domestic destinations and 25 international destinations across Asia, Europe, the Middle East and North America. Serene airline has maintained a 98.8% punctuality rate. Airblue airline Limited is a private Pakistani airline with its head office. Shaheen airline has 18 aircraft in its fleet, including Airbus A319, A320, and A330s. It offers flights to numerous domestic and international destinations. Askari Aviation Services is providing quality aviation services of helicopter and aircraft for adventure, safaris, rescue and crop dusting.

What is Tourado?

Tourado is a great Tour Planning website that helps tourists plan their trips according to their desires, budget and preferences.

Can I customize my tour?

Yes, you can surely customize your tour plan. Also you have two other ways available to plan your tour.

What are the other two ways to plan a tour using Tourado?

You can either get recommendations according to your interest and budget or select a ready-made package from the available packages.

How many traveling mediums are available to opt for?

You can select any traveling medium out of the five facilitated by Tourado. These traveling mediums include: Bus, Car, Jeep, Train and Aeroplane.

How can I book a room for myself?

You can book room(s) in hotel, take a room in guest-house on rent, or rent a room available in any house being offered by the owner of the house.
